PAPSI Certificates

This guideline was created to address the needs and requests of the PAPSI members for diverse certificates, offering clarity on the certification process and eligibility. As an organization committed to delivering top-notch, credible certifications, PAPSI takes pride in providing high-quality verified certificates. These certificates are issued in a digital format with a unique verification code, enhancing their authenticity and accessibility.

A. Certificate of Membership (comes with PAPSI ID)

This certificate is awarded to new and renewing members of PAPSI. Membership is valid for one year and can be renewed annually for Php500. It is open to all educators who wish to become members by submitting a formal application by completing the membership form at www.papsitraining.com/membership.html

B. Certificate of Participation

This certificate is awarded to the participants for their active engagement and valuable contributions during the event, recognizing their commitment to learning and knowledge sharing. It serves as a token of appreciation and encouragement for their dedication to enriching the webinar experience. The usual training cost to attend a webinar/face to face is announced. Stay updated on upcoming PAPSI webinars by visiting our official Facebook page at www.fb.com/papsifbpage. Additionally, you can access previous PAPSI webinars at www.papsitraining.com/webinar.html

C. Certificate of Completion

This certificate is awarded upon successful completion of a 3-day training series, serving as proof of the participant's accomplishment in attending all sessions. The certificate prominently displays the title of the webinar series and the total training hours completed, showcasing the participant's dedication to continuous learning and professional development. Go check and avail of our PAPSI promo - Training bundles of previous webinars, providing you with even more opportunities to enhance your knowledge and skills at www.papsitraining.com/training-bundles.html

D. Certificate of Active Engagement

Active PAPSI members who have attended three or more webinars within the year can request this certificate, which will serve as a token of appreciation and encouragement for their unwavering dedication and commitment to professional development. Your active participation in our webinars is truly valued, and this certificate is a testament to your continuous growth as an educator. You can request for this certificate by accomplishing the form at .papsitraining.com/certificate.html

E. Certificate of Appreciation

This certificate serves to recognize the invaluable contribution of the resource speaker. Their outstanding expertise, captivating presentations, and exceptional dedication as resource speakers have significantly enriched our events, and this certificate is a symbol of our appreciation.

i. as a Resource Speaker

To become a resource speaker, interested individuals can apply by submitting a formal application letter via email at papsi.seminar@gmail.com We welcome passionate and knowledgeable experts who are eager to share their expertise and contribute to our events. Your application will be carefully considered, and we look forward to potentially welcoming you as a valuable resource speaker.

F. Certificate of Recognition

This certificate is awarded to deserving awardees, teaching demonstrator, teaching demonstration observer, and teaching demonstration evaluator.

i. as an Awardee

As a valued PAPSI member and dedicated educator, your efforts will be highly recognized and appreciated. As a member, you can become an awardee by attending several training sessions, showcasing your commitment to continuous learning. For educators, being an awardee awaits you when you conduct several training sessions, exemplifying your dedication to knowledge sharing and professional development. Additionally, submit and present your research in our annual international research conference to have the opportunity to become an awardee, celebrating your contributions to advancing education and knowledge. Your outstanding achievements and active participation are integral to our collective success.

  • 1. Honorable Mention Award – attended three webinars.
  • 2. Bronze Award – attended four or five webinars.
  • 3. Silver Award – attended six to nine webinars.
  • 4. Gold Award – attended ten to nineteen webinars.
  • 5. Platinum Award – attended 20 webinars or more.
  • 6. Best Poster Presenter – the best research poster presenter at the research conference
  • 7. Best Video Presenter – the best research video presenter at the research conference
  • 8. Outstanding Educator Bronze – the speaker who conducted three webinars
  • 9. Outstanding Educator Silver – the speaker who conducted four to seven webinars
  • 10. Outstanding Educator Gold – the speaker who conducted eight to fourteen webinars
  • 11. Outstanding Educator Platinum – the speaker who conducted fifteen or more webinars

ii. as a Teaching Demonstrator

We invite experienced and skilled educators to showcase and promote best practices in science education through effective teaching demonstrations. This certificate will be awarded to educators who excel in demonstrating their innovative teaching techniques, inspiring students, and fostering a passion for science learning.

iii. as a Teaching Demonstration Observer

Participate in this enriching experience that celebrates the art of science education and empowers educators to excel in their teaching practices. To receive this certificate, become an observer and provide evaluation and feedback as you witness expert teachers demonstrate innovative and captivating pedagogies. Your insightful contributions as an observer play a crucial role in fostering a collaborative learning environment, driving professional growth for educators, and ensuring the continued enhancement of science education.

iv. as a Teaching Demonstration Evaluator

Become a teaching demonstration evaluator and earn this certificate, as you shoulder a higher level of responsibility than an observer. Your role involves closely monitoring the correctness of the content and providing valuable feedback to support the demonstrator's improvement. Your keen insights and feedback play a pivotal role in enhancing the quality of teaching demonstrations and fostering professional growth. Your dedication to ensuring excellence in science education is truly commendable, and this certificate is a token of our appreciation for your invaluable contributions to the field.

G. Certificate of Active Involvement

This certificate is awarded to PAPSI members who wholeheartedly support the organization's activities, curriculum, and programs by actively participating as members of the committee. Your commitment and dedication to contributing to the growth and success of our organization are truly commendable. This certificate serves as a token of our gratitude and appreciation for your valuable involvement and unwavering support.

i. as a member of the Program Committee

By actively participating in PAPSI activities as an emcee, moderator, or facilitator, you can earn this certificate as a recognition of your valuable involvement. Join us in making a positive impact on education and professional development through your active engagement in PAPSI activities. Your efforts are truly valued and acknowledged.

  • 1. Emcee
  • 2. Q&A Moderator
  • 3. Technical Moderator
  • 4. Facilitator

​ii. as a member of the Marketing Committee

To receive this recognition, actively promote PAPSI through social media, emails, and by recruiting new members and attendees. Be an enthusiastic advocate for our organization, seeking potential partnerships and collaboration opportunities. Your efforts in promoting PAPSI are highly valued and appreciated, and this recognition serves as a token of our gratitude for your dedication in spreading the mission and vision of our community.

  • 1. Social Media Promotion - Your tasks here are to like, follow, share, and review our social media platform to receive this certificate.
  • 2. Email Marketing - To receive this certificate, we shall actively promote PAPSI activities by sending emails to your colleagues, inviting them to participate in our events and programs.
  • 3. Membership Advocate - To receive this certificate, your role is to recruit prospects to become members of the organization.
  • 4. Collaborations and Partnerships - To receive this certificate, we invite you to explore and establish potential partnerships to promote science education. As partners, your commitment to advancing the field of science education is highly valued, and this certificate serves as a token of our appreciation for your collaboration in driving positive change.

iii. as a member of the Academic Committee

Contribute to our vibrant community by sharing your high-quality video learning content and well-structured learning modules. Your submissions will be thoroughly evaluated based on their relevance, educational value, creativity, and clarity of presentation. Once approved, your exceptional work will be proudly published on our website, making it accessible for download to our esteemed members. Additionally, you will be awarded this certificate as a distinguished recognition of your valuable contributions to our educational platform. Your dedication to enhancing the learning experience for our community is highly appreciated, and we eagerly await to showcase and share your outstanding content.

  • 1. Contribute Video Learning Content
  • 2. Contribute Learning Module
