About Us

PAPSI is a merged national organization of Physics and Science Instructors that focuses on enhancing and promoting learning specifically in the field of Natural and Physical Sciences in the Philippines. It is headed by Dr. Gil Nonato C. Santos and has a poll of experts coming from De La Salle University and other institutions.

Mission

PAPSI is geared towards the enhancement and appreciation of science through teaching. We are committed to providing the breadth and depth of understanding science in various fields such as physics, chemistry, biology, earth and environmental science, coupled with the latest pedagogical skills and effective teaching methods to improve science learning at all levels.

Vision

To be the leading professional organization that promote science education in the Philippines. We are purposely driven to produce competent and confident science educators who have a deeper appreciation of the teaching profession.

Goals

PAPSI is dedicated to advancing science education through ongoing professional development and curriculum enhancement. The organization seeks international recognition, promotes excellence in science education, and fosters collaboration to create an innovative community of science educators.

PAPSI Mobile App

The PAPSI Mobile App serves as a comprehensive platform for Physics and Science Instructors across high school, college, and graduate school levels. Functioning as a dynamic training center, PAPSI, under the leadership of Dr. Gil Nonato C. Santos and in collaboration with De La Salle University, boasts a membership of over 2,000 educators from diverse institutions. With a rich history of organizing seminars, laboratory training, workshops, and conferences, the app offers convenient features such as easy login, seamless seminar/webinar registration, membership activation, and access to a library of training materials. Additionally, it provides valuable tools for educators, including a counter, randomizer, timer, and sound effects.
